Mission of e-waste

IWB PTY LTD is dedicated to reducing the environmental and social impact of discarded electronics. We collect devices, extract reusable components, and recycle the remainder responsibly. We partner with businesses and communities to build circular-economy practices.

  • Reduce hazardous landfill waste
  • Protect your private data
  • Create local recycling jobs

To reduce the environmental impact of electronic waste by promoting responsible recycling, refurbishing, and sustainable disposal methods across communities and to educate the public on the dangers of improper electronic disposal and to provide sustainable solutions that turn waste into opportunity.To drive innovation in e-waste management through technology and education, ensuring that valuable resources are recovered and reused responsibly
